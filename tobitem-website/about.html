About Our Company

We provide a full range of Premium Industrial Services for your business


Tobitem Downhole Solution Company Ltd was formed in 2011 in Port Harcourt, Nigeria by individuals with a passion for Fishing and Drilling Tools Rental Services.

Our aim is to provide our customers with a complete fishing and drilling rental tools solution, providing:

  • High quality, reliable tools and equipment
  • Highly motivated, customer-focused personnel
  • Meticulous project engineering and job design
  • Innovative approach to product development and technological solutions

"We are committed to providing a superior level of customer service which is sometimes found lacking in today's largely profit-driven organizations."

Whatever downhole challenges you face, Tobitem would relish the opportunity to offer a cost-effective technology solution that meets your needs.

No two fishing operations are the same, but Tobitem can help you to avoid repeating past mistakes by selecting the right tool for the job and getting the job done right, first time.

We also provide drilling and fishing tool rentals — including Drilling Jars and Accelerators, Hole Openers, Stabilisers, Roller-reamers, and more — with expertise in recovery of lost and stuck pipe, junk, and lost tools both onshore and offshore.
