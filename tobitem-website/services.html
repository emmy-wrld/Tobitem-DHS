Our Services

High Quality Services

We aim to provide excellent tools and services tailored to surpass and exceed customers' expectations, driven by a well-motivated and dedicated team of professionals using the most modern technology in our industry.

Window Milling

Experienced in both bottom trip/hydraulic set single trip types of whipstocks and section milling. Our window milling operations are executed with precision to ensure optimal sidetrack positioning and bore quality.

Whether it's a standard or complex multilateral well environment, Tobitem's window milling team brings the expertise and equipment to deliver first-time success.

Whipstock Operations Section Milling Sidetrack Multilateral Wells

Milling

Casing / tubing / junk / cement milling (rotary — top drive — motors — turbines), dressing fish necks, and clearing downhole restrictions. Our milling services cover all completion scenarios across varied well types.

Tobitem's milling team is equipped to handle complex downhole obstructions with industry-proven techniques and modern milling assemblies.

Casing Milling Junk Milling Cement Milling Fish Neck Dressing

Workovers

Packer milling and retrieving (all types and sizes), bridge plugs milling and retrieving, and liner recovery — Tobitem provides comprehensive workover solutions for both production and injection wells.

Our workover services are designed to restore well integrity and production rates quickly and cost-effectively, with minimal operational downtime.

Packer Retrieval Bridge Plug Milling Liner Recovery Well Integrity

Abandonments

Onshore (multiple and single cuts) all sizes. Offshore (multiple and single cuts) all sizes. Recovery of Mud Line Hangers. Full well decommissioning compliant with regulatory requirements.

Tobitem's abandonment experts ensure every well is decommissioned safely, efficiently, and in full compliance with Nigerian and international environmental standards.

Onshore Abandonment Offshore Abandonment Mud Line Hangers Decommissioning

Oil Tool Rentals

Drilling Jars and Accelerators, Hole Openers, Stabilisers, Roller-reamers, and Thru-Tubing fishing tools in all sizes. Our rental fleet is meticulously maintained and ready for immediate mobilization.

We ensure every tool is thoroughly inspected, dressed, and certified before deployment — giving you confidence in the equipment working downhole on your operation.

Drilling Jars Accelerators Hole Openers Stabilisers Roller-reamers Thru-Tubing Tools

Manpower Supply

We supply experienced personnel to the Oil & Gas industry, including Drilling Supervisors, Completion Supervisors, Drilling Engineers, Fishing Consultants, Directional Drillers, Tool Pushers, and more.

All personnel supplied by Tobitem are vetted for technical competency, HSE compliance, and operational track record to ensure your projects benefit from the best available talent.

Drilling Supervisors Fishing Consultants Drilling Engineers Directional Drillers Tool Pushers
