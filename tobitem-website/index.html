About Our Company

Whatever Downhole Challenges You Face,
We Have the Solution

We provide full range of Premium Industrial Services for your business

Tobitem Downhole Solution Company Ltd was formed in 2011 in Port Harcourt, Nigeria by individuals with a passion for Fishing and Drilling Tools Rental Services.

Our aim is to provide our customers with a complete fishing and drilling rental tools solution — high quality, reliable tools and equipment; highly motivated, customer-focused personnel; meticulous project engineering and job design; and an innovative approach to product development.

We are committed to providing a superior level of customer service which is sometimes found lacking in today's largely profit-driven organizations.
